# ISA: VC Firm Operations Research

## Problem
User wants a maximally detailed reference on how venture capital firms actually run their operations — cadence, structure, sourcing, CRM, IC, portfolio support, LP relations, back office, tooling.

## Vision
Single synthesis document covering the full operating model of a typical institutional VC firm (seed → growth), with concrete cadences, role responsibilities, tools, and process artifacts.

## Out of Scope
- Investment thesis / strategy advice
- Returns analysis / fund math beyond ops touchpoints
- Specific firm gossip

## Principles
- Concrete > abstract: name tools, cadences, artifacts
- Cite where claims are non-obvious
- Cover both small (≤5 ppl) and platform-scale ($1B+ AUM) firms

## Constraints
- Public information only
- Synthesize from multiple primary sources (firm blogs, OpenLP, NVCA, practitioner writeups)

## Goal
Deliver a structured operational playbook in the chat.

## Criteria
- ISC-1: Covers deal sourcing & outreach
- ISC-2: Covers pipeline/CRM management
- ISC-3: Covers investment process / IC
- ISC-4: Covers portfolio management & platform
- ISC-5: Covers LP relations & fundraising ops
- ISC-6: Covers firm structure, roles, comp, cadence
- ISC-7: Covers back office (fund admin, legal, finance, compliance)
- ISC-8: Covers tooling stack

## Test Strategy
Self-review: each ISC matched to a section in synthesis.

## Features
Synthesized markdown report delivered in conversation.

## Decisions
- Use parallel research agents per domain to maximize depth.

## Changelog
- Created ISA for E5 research task.
- Appended E4 hook-system retrospective lessons.

## Verification
- ISC-1 (sourcing & outreach): Part 1 — funnel, channels, scouts, demo days, communities, conferences, conversion benchmarks.
- ISC-2 (CRM/pipeline): Part 2 — Affinity/DealCloud/Attio map, stages, MPM, data ownership, notes/memos, integration pipe, founder tracker, fields, portco data flow.
- ISC-3 (investment process/IC): Part 3 — MPM cadence, timelines, sequence, authority models, memo structure, diligence workstreams, legal/financial DD, TS, closing, pass culture, anti-portfolio.
- ISC-4 (portfolio/platform): Part 4 — board cadence, KPIs by sector, PRC, ASC 820 marks, reserves/follow-ons, bridges, down rounds, secondaries, platform functions (talent/GTM/marketing/community/capmarkets/exit).
- ISC-5 (LP relations/fundraise): Part 5 — segmentation table, raise timeline, data room, ref calls, LP CRM, ILPA reporting, AGM, capital calls, distributions, side letters, co-invest, LPAC, full regulatory.
- ISC-6 (structure/comp/cadence): Part 6 — headcount, roles, comp table with carry pts, carry economics, title track, weekly + annual cadence, decision rights, conflict, recruiting, DEI, remote.
- ISC-7 (back office/compliance): Part 7 — entity stack, admin scope + cost, ASC 946 accounting, ASC 820 valuation, audit, tax (UBTI/ECI/QSBS), RIA/ERA/Form PF/Marketing Rule, AML, insurance, expenses, banking, IT, sub-docs, cap-table, counsel, Rule 204-2.
- ISC-8 (tooling): Part 8 — 20-category vendor map + canonical small and mid/large stack profiles + total spend.
